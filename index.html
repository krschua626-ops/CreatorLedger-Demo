FAQ

Questions worth answering.

How does CreatorLedger get my revenue data? ▼
In Phase 1, you enter revenue manually — takes about 5 minutes per month. In Phase 2 (Months 4–6), we integrate directly with YouTube Analytics, Patreon, and Plaid for bank feeds. We never store passwords — only OAuth tokens, encrypted at rest.
Is the tax estimate actually accurate? ▼
It's built on real IRS self-employment tax rates and validated with licensed CPAs. We position it as an estimate, not advice — but it's significantly more accurate than guessing. We also flag common deductions creators miss.
I'm not that big yet. Is this for me? ▼
The free tier was built for exactly this. If you're earning across more than one platform and losing track of brand deal payments, CreatorLedger will save you time immediately — no minimum income required. The paid tiers make sense once automation starts saving you real hours per month.
How is this different from QuickBooks or Wave? ▼
QuickBooks was designed for law firms and restaurants. It has no concept of AdSense payout schedules, Patreon membership revenue, or brand deal payment terms. You'd spend more time configuring QuickBooks than earning. CreatorLedger is purpose-built — it knows what a YouTube CPM is, and it knows Net-30 means your brand deal payment is late.
