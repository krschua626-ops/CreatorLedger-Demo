FAQ

Questions worth answering.

How does CreatorLedger get my revenue data? ▼
In Phase 1, you enter revenue manually — takes about 5 minutes per month. In Phase 2 we connect directly with YouTube Analytics, Patreon, and Plaid for bank feeds via OAuth. We never store your passwords, only encrypted access tokens.
Is the tax estimate accurate? ▼
It's built on real IRS self-employment tax rates and validated with licensed CPAs. We position it as an estimate, not advice — but it's significantly more accurate than guessing, and it catches deductions most creators miss entirely.
I'm not that big yet. Is this for me? ▼
The free tier was built for exactly this stage. If you're earning across more than one platform and losing track of brand deal payments, CreatorLedger will save you time immediately — no minimum income required.
How is this different from QuickBooks? ▼
QuickBooks was built for law firms and restaurants. It has no concept of AdSense payout schedules, Patreon membership tiers, or Net-30 brand deal terms. You'd spend more time configuring it than using it. CreatorLedger knows what a YouTube CPM is.
What's the long-term roadmap? ▼
Dashboard and sponsorship tracking first, then API integrations, then revenue-based lending and a creator business account — the financial infrastructure banks can't offer because they don't understand creator income. Think Brex, but for the creator economy.
